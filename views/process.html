How we work?

One of the most important services we provide is transpanrency and communication.
Our service is therefore different from those others provide. While others just want a contract, which they can simply work off, relies our service on a very feedback based system, to provide the perfect solution without any misunderstandings or unwanted, expensive features.

A Project Process

Talking about reliability as one of our key values, you need to know of course what we rely on.
This is how the usual process of a project with us looks like.

RFP Submission

Simply submit a request for project (RFP) telling us the key areas where your company requires help in and we will look into it!

Requirement elicitation

Upon recieving your RFP, we will carry out the necessary research, come up with solutions and prepare a pitch.

Initial meeting and Pitch

Set aside some time to discuss the details of the project and hear how our solution will be beneficial for you.

Reiteration and changes

Based on the information received during the meeting, we will make the necessary changes and send you the final proposal.

Acceptance of project

Once the proposal is satisfactory, send the go signal for us to begin providing our services.

Execution

Our team will either work remotely or at your office where necessary to complete the required tasks at satisfactory standards.

Feedback

Your feedback on the quality and timeliness on our services are welcomed at any time throughout the project.

Completion

Marks the end of the project period!
